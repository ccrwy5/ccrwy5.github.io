About Me


Biography

Welcome! My name is Chris Rehagen and I am a current student at the University of Missouri studying Information Technology and Computer Science. I am currently in my third year of school and over these past few years, I have come to know and love the areas of Web Development and iOS App Development. For me, there is just something special about creating unique user interfaces and apps that can serve a specific function but also have a appealing aesthetic appearance.


On the Web Dev front, for starters, this website in and of itself is a bit of a passion project of mine and I've used it as practice in launching, hosting and maintaining my own site. Beyond using the obvious HTML5 and CSS3, I have experience using a variety of frameworks including Bootstrap, Pure, Vue, and Angular. I'm looking to expand my knowledge and skills with Angular in the future as well as learn React and Ruby, seeing as those are incredibly popular and widely used in the industry today


Now, onto the iOS front. iOS Developement is something I've just recently been getting into within the last 5 months and I can confidently say it's something I'm going to consider studying, playing with, and diving deeper into in the coming years. Similar to what drew me into web development, I find it incredibly satisfing to see a good looking visual of something I've created and giving it function through the code I write.


On a quick and final note, I want to thank you for exploring my website and checking out my work and passions in this field. If you would like to get into contact with me, please visit my contact page!


Thank you again,

Christopher Rehagen
